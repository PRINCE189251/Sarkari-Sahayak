About Sarkari Sahayak

Your Trusted Partner in Accessing Government Schemes

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between government initiatives and citizens by providing clear, concise, and accessible information about various schemes and programs in both Hindi and English languages. We aim to empower every Indian citizen with the knowledge to access benefits they are entitled to.

Our Vision

To create an empowered India where every citizen can easily access and benefit from government schemes designed for their welfare and development. We envision a nation where no eligible person misses out on government benefits due to lack of information or complexity in processes.

Who We Are

Sarkari Sahayak is an initiative to make government schemes easier to understand and access for every citizen of India. We believe that every individual should have easy access to information about government schemes that can benefit them. Our platform serves as a comprehensive guide to help citizens navigate through the complex landscape of government initiatives.


We simplify complex government documents, eligibility criteria, application processes, and required documents into easy-to-understand language. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that the information provided is accurate, up-to-date, and relevant to our users' needs.
